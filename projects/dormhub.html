Dormhub — Dormitory Management System

PHP • HTML • CSS • JavaScript • MySQL

Features & Functions

Tenant Registration & Tracking

  • Efficient Tenant Management: Dormhub allows administrators to quickly register tenants and track their room assignments accurately. Each tenant profile includes detailed personal information and payment history.
  • Streamlined Workflow: The system helps admins avoid double-booking rooms and ensures all tenant information is centralized for easy access.

Payment Management & Invoicing

  • Automated Payment Tracking: Dormhub handles all tenant payments efficiently and generates invoices automatically, reducing errors.
  • Comprehensive Records: Each payment is logged and easily auditable, helping maintain transparency and accountability.

Room & Availability Management

  • Dynamic Room Allocation: Admins can add, edit, and monitor room availability in real-time, ensuring smooth tenant assignments.
  • Conflict Prevention: The system prevents double-booking and tracks room occupancy efficiently for better dormitory management.

Reports & Analytics

  • Detailed Occupancy Reports: Dormhub generates accurate occupancy and payment reports, giving admins insights into dorm usage.
  • Data-Driven Decisions: Visual dashboards provide analytics for trend monitoring and help optimize dormitory management operations.
